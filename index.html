Vogue

Vogue - specifically Vogue Fem, is a style of dance that originated in the US created by black and brown queer individuals. It's been influencing pop culture for decades starting from Madonnas pop song titled "Vogue" which was about Vogue Fem

Artists

Artists who's works have a strong focus on the joy their rich cultures bring them are always a big inspiration. Examples like Beyonce, Rina Sawayama and Ethel Cain.

Runways

Runways are an art of collaboration between so many artists ranging from makeup artists to designers to even audio engineers that all make a runway what it is. My favourite runway is Mugler 1997.
